Welcome

Welcome to Hark the Sound, a collection of free audio games on the web. Our games have speech, sound, and graphics suitable for children with visual impairments but appealing to players of all ages and abilities.

Choose a Game

You can browse and search our game catalog like any other website. But our catalog page is also self-voicing. Kids can choose their own games to play by listening and using the arrow keys.
